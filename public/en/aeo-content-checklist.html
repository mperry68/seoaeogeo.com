AEO Content Checklist

A step-by-step checklist to optimize your content for AI-powered search engines and voice assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing content so that AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude can easily find, understand, and cite your content when answering user queries. As conversational AI becomes the primary way people search, AEO is essential for modern content strategies.
