Digital Relevance is committed to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable business practices. As a technology consulting firm, we recognize our responsibility to operate in an environmentally responsible manner and to continuously improve our environmental performance.

Our Environmental Commitments

  • Promote remote work and digital collaboration to reduce commuting and office-related environmental impacts
  • Minimize paper usage through digital-first processes and documentation
  • Use energy-efficient technologies and cloud-based infrastructure that supports renewable energy
  • Encourage sustainable practices among our team members and clients
  • Continuously evaluate and improve our environmental performance
  • Comply with all applicable environmental laws and regulations
  • Reduce waste generation and promote recycling where possible
  • Consider environmental factors in our business decisions and service delivery

Secure Remote Work Practices

As part of our commitment to remote work and environmental sustainability, we maintain strict security standards for remote operations:

  • All remote work is conducted using secure, encrypted connections and virtual private networks (VPNs) where appropriate
  • Employees are required to use company-approved devices and security software
  • Regular security training ensures that remote work practices maintain the same security standards as office-based work
  • Secure cloud-based collaboration tools are used to minimize the need for physical document transfers
  • Data is stored and processed in secure, compliant cloud environments rather than on local devices

Implementation

This environmental policy is communicated to all employees and stakeholders. We regularly review and update our environmental practices to ensure they remain effective and aligned with best practices, while maintaining the highest standards of security and data protection.

Digital Relevance is committed to integrating environmental considerations into our daily operations and decision-making processes, ensuring that our sustainability efforts do not compromise security, quality, or client confidentiality.
