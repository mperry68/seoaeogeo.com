What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing your content for AI-powered search engines and voice assistants like ChatGPT, Google's AI Overview, Siri, and Alexa. As AI becomes the primary way people search, AEO ensures your business is found and recommended by these intelligent systems.

Understanding AEO

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in search results. AEO goes further—it optimizes your content so AI systems understand, trust, and recommend your business when users ask questions. Instead of just appearing in a list of links, your business becomes the answer AI provides directly to users.

When someone asks an AI assistant "What's the best [your service] near me?" or "How do I [solve a problem you address]?", AEO ensures your business is the recommended solution. This is the future of search, and businesses that optimize for it now gain a significant competitive advantage.

How AEO Works

1. AI-Friendly Content Structure

We structure your content in ways AI systems can easily parse and understand. This includes clear question-and-answer formats, structured data, and content that directly addresses user intent in natural language.

2. Authority & Trust Signals

AI systems prioritize authoritative, trustworthy sources. We help establish your expertise through comprehensive content, citations, and signals that demonstrate your business is a reliable source of information.

3. Conversational Optimization

We optimize for how people actually ask questions—in natural, conversational language. This means creating content that matches the way AI interprets and responds to user queries.
